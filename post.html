Jenna Gardiner - Hobbies

Below are some of my favorite hobbies:





  • Singing
    1. I've practically been singing nonstop since I could speak.
    2. I've taken every opportunity I can get to join choirs since I was in 2nd grade.
    3. I participated in Solo and Ensemble every year in high school, even making it to state.
    4. Some of my favorite genres of music are soundtrack, country, pop, latino pop, and a capella.

A song I wrote for a Book of Mormon class final project my freshman year at BYU (sorry for the terrible quality)





  • Acting
    1. Growing up in Alaska, I didn't have many opportunities to act in plays until high school, but once I got to high school, I joined every play I could.
    2. Throughout my high school career, I performed in many plays, including Oz, Once Upon a Mattress, An Absolutely True Story As Told By A Bunch Of Lying Liars, Into the Woods, and High School Musical: On Stage!
    3. Some of my favorite roles included Gabriella Montez, Barney (luckily not the dinosaur), Lady Larken, Eli, and Rah.
    4. My ultimate dream in life is to be in a Hallmark Christmas movie.

Poster for High School Musical: On Stage!





  • Adventuring in Alaska
    1. Every time summer roles around in Alaska, my friends and I love lake days, including jet skiing, wakeboarding, rope swinging, kayaking, and jumping into the lake.
    2. My favorite thing to do in Alaska is go to the mudflats and slide down the mud into the water.
    3. Every summer, my friends and I plan a backpacking trip, my favorite being Symphony and Eagle Lakes.
    4. When winter roles around, I always enjoy snow machining, skiing, ice skating, sledding, and making snow angles.
